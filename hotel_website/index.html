The History Of Our Hotel

SAS International Hotels signed a franchise agreement with Carlson Hotels to manage the Radisson brand in EMEA, which would later become Radisson Blu. We have more than 75 years of hospitality experience. With that experience, Radisson Hotel Group has grown to become one of the largest hotel companies in the industry with a powerful set of brands that deliver world class hospitality and exceptional service.
